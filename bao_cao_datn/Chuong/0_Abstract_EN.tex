In the era of the digital economy, the Customer-to-Customer (C2C) business model is growing rapidly, especially in the field of second-hand book trading. However, the risks of fraud and lack of transaction transparency remain the biggest barriers for users. This report presents the research and development of a "C2C E-commerce Platform for Books" integrated with an Escrow payment mechanism.

The system is built on Next.js, Prisma, and MySQL, combined with the VNPay Sandbox payment gateway. Key solutions include multi-shop order splitting, cash flow management using a Ledger system, and a payment-freezing mechanism to protect buyers. Experimental results show that the system operates stably, ensuring financial safety and transparency in all transactions, effectively meeting the requirements of a modern e-commerce platform.

\textbf{Keywords:} C2C E-commerce, Escrow Payment, Next.js, Prisma, Second-hand books.
