    \textbf{\huge ABSTRACT}
\end{center}
\vspace{0.5cm}

The thesis focuses on researching and developing a specialized e-commerce platform for second-hand books based on the C2C (Customer-to-Customer) model. The core differentiator of the system lies in the Escrow payment mechanism and the ability to process multi-seller shopping carts.

In terms of technology, the project utilizes Next.js as the foundational framework, Prisma ORM for MySQL database management, and integrates the VNPay payment gateway. The achieved result is a stable system that allows users to freely exchange books, ensures financial safety for buyers, and provides transparent revenue management for sellers through an internal e-wallet system. The thesis not only resolves technical challenges regarding cart splitting but also contributes a practical solution for the book-loving community in Vietnam.

\textbf{Keywords:} C2C, Bookstore, Escrow, Next.js, Prisma, Intermediate Payment.
